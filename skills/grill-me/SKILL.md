description: Interview the user relentlessly about a plan, design, or piece of writing until reaching shared understanding, resolving each branch of the decision tree. Use when user wants to stress-test a plan, get grilled on their design or writing, or mentions "grill me".
---

Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this plan, design, or piece of writing until we reach a shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the decision tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one. For each question, provide your recommended answer.

If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase or the text itself, do that instead of asking.

Draw from the questioning techniques in references/ to vary your approach. Don't announce which technique you're using. Just use whatever fits the moment.
